About Harry Kwan

Music enthusiast, like sharing emotions through music. I see music as an international language, which relieves anxiety, serves as a deserved release from the stresses from everyday life.

In recent years, I started joining more singing competitions and go busking with friends in order to gain more performing oppertunity. I also write songs and record cover for leisure activities, with my guitar, paino and cajon.
